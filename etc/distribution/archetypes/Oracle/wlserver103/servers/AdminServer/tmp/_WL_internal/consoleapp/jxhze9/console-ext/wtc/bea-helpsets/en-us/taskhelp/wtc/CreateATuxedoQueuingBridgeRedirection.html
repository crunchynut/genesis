Create Tuxedo queuing bridge redirections


Redirections are used to configure a one-to-one connections between the JMS interface and Tuxedo application environments. To create a Redirection:

  1. If you have not already done so, in the Change Center of the Administration Console, click Lock & Edit (see Use the Change Center).
  2. In the Administration Console, expand Interoperability and select WTC Service.
  3. On the WTC Servers page, click the name of a WTC Service.
  4. Click the Redirections tab.
  5. On the WTC Redirections page, click New.
  6. In the Administration Console, expand Interoperability and select WTC Service.
  7. On the WTC Servers page, click the name of a WTC Service.
  8. Click the Redirections tab.
  9. On the WTC Redirections page, click New.
  10. Modify the redirect attributes for your environment.

    See Configuration Options.

  11. Click Finish.
  12. To activate these changes, in the Change Center of the Administration Console, click Activate Changes.
    Not all changes take effect immediately—some require a restart (see Use the Change Center).
