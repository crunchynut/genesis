Configure connection factories for queuing bridges


To define the configuration of connection factories for use with this Tuxedo queuing bridge:

  1. If you have not already done so, in the Change Center of the Administration Console, click Lock & Edit (see Use the Change Center).
  2. In the Administration Console, expand Interoperability and select WTC Service.
  3. On the WTC Servers page, click the name of a WTC Service.
  4. Click the Queuing Bridge tab.
  5. On the WTC Tuxedo Queuing Bridges page, click the name of the WTC service's queuing bridge.
  6. Click the Factories tab.
  7. Modify the factory attributes for your environment.

    See Configuration Options.

  8. Click Save.
  9. To activate these changes, in the Change Center of the Administration Console, click Activate Changes.
    Not all changes take effect immediately—some require a restart (see Use the Change Center).
